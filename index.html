slncky

About

slncky is a tool that filters a set of gene annotations into a set of bonafide long noncoding RNAs (lncRNAs) and searches for orthologous genes in another species using a sensitive non-coding aligning method. Noncoding gene alignments can be visualized in our Compare Browser. Alignments of conserved pluripotent lncRNAs from our paper can be visualized at our Pluripotent lncRNA Compare Browser.

slncky was developed as a collaboration between the Garber Lab at UMass Medical and the Regev Lab at the Broad Institute.
